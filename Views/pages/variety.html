Inter Variety

Inter variety show is an event where the variety team of other colleges can participate. A reputed judge will be invited to judge and award marks for their performance.

Inta Variety

Intra variety show is an event where all the unofficial variety teams of CEG can perform. A theme will be given to all the teams and the teams have to prepare tracks accordingly.
